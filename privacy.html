Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2026

Your Conversations Are Private: We do not and cannot access the content of your conversations with your Moltbot instance. Your messages are processed directly between your messaging platform and Anthropic's API. We only access technical information needed to maintain the service.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Moltbot ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our Service.

By using Moltbot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Account Information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Billing information (processed by Stripe; we do not store credit card details)

Service Configuration:

  • Your Anthropic API key (encrypted and stored securely)
  • Messaging platform credentials (e.g., phone number for WhatsApp, Telegram user ID)
  • Preferred messaging platform selection

Communications:

  • Support emails and correspondence
  • Setup call notes (if applicable)

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

Technical Logs:

  • Server uptime and availability metrics
  • Error logs (technical errors, not message content)
  • API connection status
  • Server resource usage (CPU, memory, disk)

Usage Information:

  • Date of service activation
  • Subscription status and billing history
  • Basic analytics (number of messages sent, not content)

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect or have access to:

  • The content of your conversations with the bot
  • Messages you send to your bot
  • Responses generated by Claude
  • Conversation history or context
  • Any personal information contained in your messages

Technical Explanation: Your bot instance processes messages directly between your messaging platform and Anthropic's API. We configure the infrastructure but do not intercept, log, or store message content.

5.3 Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for:

6.1 Active Accounts

We retain your information for as long as your account is active and you continue to use the Service.

After you cancel your subscription or we terminate your account:

Since we do not collect conversation data, there is no conversation history to delete from our systems. Your conversation history (if any) exists only:
