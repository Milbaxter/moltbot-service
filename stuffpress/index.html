Frequently Asked Questions

What is StuffPress?

StuffPress is a hosted AI assistant service. We set up and run your own private Moltbot instance on a cloud server, so you get a personal AI assistant that actually handles your stuff ‚Äî not just answers questions.

What is Moltbot?

Moltbot is an open-source AI assistant platform that runs on your own devices. It's what developers and power users install when they need an AI that can actually DO things ‚Äî run code, process files, connect to apps, automate workflows. Think of it as the difference between a chatbot (just talks) and a real assistant (actually handles stuff).

Do I need technical knowledge to use StuffPress?

Nope! That's the point. Moltbot normally requires technical setup (installing Node.js, configuring servers, managing API keys, etc.). We handle all of that for you. You just text your stuff on WhatsApp or Telegram. No technical knowledge needed.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. Your StuffPress instance runs on YOUR private cloud server, completely isolated from other users. Your conversations, files, and data stay on your server. We set it up and manage it, but your stuff is yours. We don't train AI models on your data, and we don't share it with anyone.

What can StuffPress actually do?

Because StuffPress is powered by Moltbot, it can run code, process files, connect to your calendar and email, schedule tasks, browse the web, create automations, and much more. It's like having your own computer in the cloud that's always working on your stuff. Unlike chatbots (which just answer questions), StuffPress actually handles things.

How is this different from ChatGPT or Claude?

ChatGPT/Claude: Passive chatbots. You go to their website, type a message, get a reply. Conversations disappear. No memory. Can't do anything outside the chat window.

StuffPress: Active assistant with its own computer. Lives in WhatsApp/Telegram (apps you already use). Remembers everything. Can run code, process files, build automations. Works 24/7 on your stuff even when you're not chatting.

Yes! Advanced users can bring their own API keys. This gives you direct control over AI costs and lets you choose which models to use. We'll help you configure it during setup.
